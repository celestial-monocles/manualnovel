The tool automatically detects chapters and organizes them in the Table of Contents dropdown menu. If automatic detection fails, use the Manual Chapter Split button (✚ icon) to split by custom patterns.

Features:

  • Auto-saves reading position every 5 minutes
  • Bookmarks your last read chapter (persists until manually cleared or browser data is cleared)
  • Caches files for faster reloading
  • Dark/Warm/Light themes
  • Customizable fonts and reading settings
  • Find & Replace: Load custom JSON rules for bulk changes, or search directly within this chapter.
  • NEW: Manual chapter detection for custom patterns
  • NEW: Clear cache & data option in Settings

Limitations:

  • Files over 50MB may not load properly
  • Complex formatting (images, tables, special characters) in .docx files may not display correctly
  • Bookmarks and cache are browser-specific
  • Cache automatically clears after 24 hours
